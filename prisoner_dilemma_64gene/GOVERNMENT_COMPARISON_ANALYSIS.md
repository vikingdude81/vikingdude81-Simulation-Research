## ðŸ”¬ SCIENTIFIC FINDINGS

### 1. **Authoritarian Government: 99.9% Cooperation**

**Policy**: Remove all defectors (wealth â†’ -9999)

**Result**: Nearly perfect cooperation achieved through forced compliance.

**Mechanism**:
- Defectors immediately removed from population
- Creates artificial selection pressure favoring cooperation
- Cooperation becomes only viable strategy
- Population survives but genetic diversity likely reduced

**Interpretation**:
- **Most effective** for maximizing cooperation
- Achieves cooperation through elimination, not evolution
- Questions: Is this sustainable? What's the cost to diversity?

**Real-World Parallel**: Totalitarian regimes with strict enforcement

---

### 2. **Welfare State: 57.1% Cooperation (+12%)**

**Policy**: Tax 30% from agents with wealth >50, redistribute to agents with wealth <10

**Result**: Significant cooperation increase through economic redistribution.

**Mechanism**:
- Redistribution helps cooperators survive tough times
- Reduces inequality, allowing diverse strategies to coexist
- Economic safety net promotes risk-taking (cooperation)
- Natural selection still operates, but with modified fitness landscape

**Interpretation**:
- **Moderate improvement** through economic intervention
- Balances cooperation with genetic diversity
- Proves redistribution can shift evolutionary equilibria

**Real-World Parallel**: Nordic welfare states, social safety nets

---

### 3. **Laissez-Faire: 45.1% Cooperation (Baseline)**

**Policy**: No government intervention, pure natural selection

**Result**: Natural equilibrium with moderate cooperation.

**Mechanism**:
- Free market dynamics
- Cooperators and defectors coexist
- Natural prisoner's dilemma equilibrium
- Pure evolutionary forces

**Interpretation**:
- **Baseline reference point**
- Shows natural cooperation level without intervention
- Represents "default" evolutionary outcome

**Real-World Parallel**: Free market capitalism, minimal government

---

### 4. **Central Banker: *Running***

**Policy**: +10 wealth stimulus when average wealth <15

**Prediction**: 55-65% cooperation

**Mechanism**:
- Economic stabilization prevents collapse
- Stimulus during downturns supports all agents
- Prevents defection spirals during resource scarcity

**Expected Outcome**: Modest improvement through economic stability

---

### 5. **Mixed Economy: *Running***

**Policy**: Adaptive switching between policies based on conditions

**Prediction**: 65-75% cooperation (best overall)

**Mechanism**:
- Dynamic policy selection
- Welfare during inequality, stimulus during scarcity, laissez-faire during prosperity
- Optimization through adaptation

**Expected Outcome**: Best balance of cooperation and efficiency
